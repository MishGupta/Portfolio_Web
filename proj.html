Burger Shack Ordering System

A C++ program designed to streamline food ordering for a restaurant. It offers a customizable menu, payment options (card, cash, UPI, Swiggy One), and additional features like order confirmation, feedback, and customer detail inputs.

Gym Management System

A comprehensive tool developed with MySQL and NetBeans to manage gym operations. It handles member registration, scheduling, program categorization, and maintains a database for staff and trainers.

Travel Itinerary Generator

A platform for users to create personalized travel plans, featuring AI-driven itineraries, payment options, and secure user authentication. It offers a "Surprise-Me" feature to make spontaneous travel plans.

Jivadaya App

It is designed to streamline the operations of temple book stalls. It addresses key challenges such as team management, purchase tracking, payment processing, inventory management, and invoicing. The app provides a user-friendly platform for tracking sales, managing stock, and ensuring smooth transactions, ultimately helping volunteers and temple staff run the book stalls more efficiently.
